Augmented Reality

Vermax Industries is always working to define a framework for the interoperability of Augmented Reality (AR) components, systems and services. The AR framework defines an overall high-level architecture and also identify key components and interfaces required for an AR solution.

We are capable of building augmented reality apps which are capable of:

  • Scanning real-life surroundings and perceiving them as an image on an XYZ coordinate system. We always ensure the positioning of 3D models which is based on accurately calculated three-dimensional plane rules of a perceived image, which makes augmentations appear highly realistic and natural.
  • Recognizing specific 2D images and 3D real-life objects and can augment them with the relevant text or model data on the device screen.
  • Dynamically resizing and calibrating augmentations on the screen as you and your device move around.
  • Automatically adjusting the lighting of the 3D model to naturally match that of the scene in the camera lens.

Some AR apps we build include:

  • Training and educational AR apps
  • Tourism and culture AR apps
  • Scientific AR apps
